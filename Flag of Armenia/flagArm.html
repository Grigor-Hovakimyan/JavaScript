National flag of Armenia

What do the Colors of the Flag Signify?

Red Color


Red color indicates the hardships and bloody path, which Armenians have faced during their history. The red color is mainly associated with Armenian Genocide, the innocent victims massacred by Ottoman Empire in 1915.

Blue Color

Blue stands for a pure sky and peace, which has always been a desire for every Armenian. This color is also associated with freshness and unique beauty of Armenian nature.

Orange Color

Orange color indicated the hardworking nature and braveness of Armenian nation.
